Cadence is a personal cycling and running companion app.

This is a single-developer hobby project; you can read all the source
code at https://github.com/yschimke/cadence.

Notes:
- The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to
  the developer.
- No analytics, no advertising, no crash-reporting SDKs.
- Settings and cached state stay on your device.

Privacy policy: https://yschimke.github.io/cadence/privacy/
Issues / feedback: https://github.com/yschimke/cadence/issues
